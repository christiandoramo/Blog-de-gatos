Are Cats Astronauts?

Cats are fascinating animals surrounded by mysteries. With independent and mysterious temperaments, these felines are known for their agility, elegance, and often unpredictable behavior. However, recently, a theory has gained traction on the internet and among conspiracy theorists: are cats astronauts?
The theory suggests that cats are not terrestrial animals but extraterrestrial beings sent to perform a specific mission on our planet. Historical accounts point to the fact that cats were considered sacred beings in different cultures and even associated with divine figures.
This could indicate that cats have a much more mystical origin than previously thought.
Additionally, there are theorists who believe that felines have telepathic abilities and can communicate with other alien species. This belief is based on reports of people claiming to have seen cats reacting to stimuli that are not perceptible to humans or even behaving unusually during astronomical events, such as an aurora borealis. These and other clues have been interpreted as signs that cats could be interplanetary beings.
